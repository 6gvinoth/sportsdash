Cricket Rules

1.The match will be played with a hard red tennis ball (Brand: Vicky red tennis ball)

2. Each team should use at least 16 players across the tournament (4 matches) of which  5(minimum) players shouldn’t have played the tournament in last two years.

3 .Scenario: In case a team only used  11 players (everyone appeared the cricket tournament in the year past) in three matches, then its mandate to use all 5 new players in the last match.

4. In case of a shortage of new players then the team should play with 6 + available new players count in the above scenario. No excuse will be given.

5.The match will be for 12 overs. Minimum of 5 bowlers should be used[ With two bowler can bowl a maximum of 3 overs each]

6. Field restriction of 6 in and 5 out will be followed.

7. Scenario: For the team, playing a match with a shortage of X players, then inner circle restriction (6 in and 5-X out ) should be given a higher preference.

8. Toss will be thrown sharply at 10:15 AM on the match day and match will begin at 10:30 AM[ Given there is no barrier to start the play ]

9. After the toss, the representative of a team who wins toss must call his decision immediately before he/she steps out.[Meaning no time will be given for team discussion after the toss to make a call]

10. A maximum of 15 minutes break will be given between the innings.

11.Umpire decision will be the final decision.[No more views will be encouraged ]

12. 12 man squad details should be provided at the toss time to the officials.

13. Match will not be delayed due to a shortage of players in a team, play will start with available players.

14. In the above case, the team can play with substitutes players but players can stand in the field up to 6 overs max, after which the team should play with available players listed in the squad.[No changes will be accepted after a ball being bowled]

15. The player joining in the middle of a match can bowl after one over wait after his/her entry into the playing field.

16. The rescheduling of the match will happen in case of an unplayable condition like weather and some external disturbance. [ Rescheduling will not be accepted in term of any internal team problems]

17. In case of any internal team issue, it's their wish to play the match or to give a walkover.[ Match will not be rescheduled at any cost]

18. After Bidding, before cricket tournament starts each team must assign at least 4 members to do umpiring throughout the tournament. A day before the match day third team must reveal 3 names from the assigned list who will do umpiring on the match day (before EOD).

19. Assigned umpires are subjected to change in case of an emergency situation. But three umpires is a must for a match day.
